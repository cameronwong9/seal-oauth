Privacy Policy

Effective: February 15, 2026

Seal ("we," "our," or "us") is a mobile application that helps couples track shared expenses, split costs, and settle balances. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create a Seal account, we collect your email address, display name, and password. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text.

Financial Data via Plaid: When you connect a bank account through Plaid, we receive transaction data including merchant name, amount, date, and category. We only request the "transactions" product scope. We do not access or store your bank login credentials — those are handled exclusively by Plaid.

Application Data: We store data you create within the app, including tabs, expense assignments, settlement records, and activity logs.

Device Information: We collect your device's push notification token (Expo Push Token) to deliver notifications. We do not collect device fingerprints, advertising identifiers, or usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the Seal app:

  • Displaying your bank transactions for expense tracking
  • Calculating shared expenses and balances between partners
  • Sending push notifications about account activity (new transactions, settlement requests)
  • Authenticating your identity and protecting your account

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We do not serve ads within the app.

3. Data Sharing

Your financial data is shared only with your designated partner within your Seal household. We share data with the following service providers, solely to operate the app:

  • Plaid: Bank account linking and transaction retrieval. Plaid's privacy policy is available at plaid.com/legal.
  • Supabase: Database hosting, user authentication, and server-side functions. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.
  • Expo: Push notification delivery only. No financial data is included in notification payloads.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your financial data seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure you can only access your own partnership's data
  • API keys and Plaid access tokens are stored server-side and never exposed to the mobile app
  • TOTP-based multi-factor authentication is available for all accounts
  • Our infrastructure provider (Supabase) maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and necessary to provide our services. Plaid access tokens are retained only while the linked bank account is active. When you delete your account, we permanently delete all your data within 30 days, including revoking all Plaid access tokens.

Accounts that are inactive for 12 consecutive months may be flagged for deletion after a 30-day notice period.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update your personal information through the app or by contacting us
  • Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Data Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format (JSON or CSV)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at cameronwong9@gmail.com. We will respond to requests within 30 calendar days.

7. Children's Privacy

Seal is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will delete that information promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via the app or email. The "Effective" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last updated.
